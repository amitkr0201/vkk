Vishwam Kathak Kendra

Spreading Kathak Across the UK

Our Goal

Vishwam Kathak Kendra's goal is to spread the joy of Kathak all over UK. Established in UK in 2017, Vishwam Kathak Kendra has more than 100 students, varying from beginner to advanced.

Priya Kushwaha (Founder)

Vishwam Kathak Kendra was founded by Priya Kushwaha, a renowned UK based Kathak artist with her roots of Allahabad. She showed her interest in dance even when she was young enough to not properly stand. She always performed dance in her school cultural festivals. She has also won many dance competitions at district level. She won her first solo award at the age of 7.
