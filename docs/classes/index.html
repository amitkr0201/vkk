Classes

Grades

Students from all grades; Pre-grade (Praveshika) to Grade 7; are taught. Classes are grouped according to grades with best effort, but might require combining depending on the count of students in that batch.

Time Table

Rough time-table of classes is as below

Day of Week Time of Day Location
Saturday Morning Harrow Arts Centre
Sunday Morning Harrow Arts Centre
Sunday (Last of Month) Evening Heston Community School
Monday Evening Harrow Arts Centre
All other days Evening Online (over Zoom)

Examinations

Students are taught in order to train them for Graded examinations. Examinations are conducted by Griffin College London which provides UCAS Points for later grades as well.
