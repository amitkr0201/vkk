Priya Kushwaha

Born in 1989, with no classical background Priya started her Kathak journey on August 23, 2002 under the tutelage of Pt. Prahlad Bhatt ji and finished her Prabhakar from Prayag Sangeet Samiti in 2009, Kathak Visharad from Bhatkhande Sangeet Vidyapeeth Lucknow in 2010.

In her Kathak journey she came to know Guru Urmila Sharma and started her further learning in Lucknow Gharana. Under Guru Urmila Sharma's guidance she achieved Gold medal in Praveen (8th year; equivalent to Masters) all India level examination in 2012.

Kathak not only remain her hobby but has developed into her passion. She has performed on many reputed stages like Prayag Sangeet Samiti, North Central Zone Cultural Centre (NCZCC), Triveni Mahotsav, Bhopal Mahotsav Madhya Pradesh, Doordarshan and in 2016 she performed in High commission of Maldives.
